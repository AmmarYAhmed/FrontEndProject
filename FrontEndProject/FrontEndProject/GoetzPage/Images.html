Projection Room Showcase


The projection room began as film-based, featuring a variety of technologies: flat & scope lenses, a digital reader, a plotts platter system, a lamphouse, and others.


The current room features a 3000 watt xenon bulb and programming screen. The digital server supplements with an internal portable hard drive.
